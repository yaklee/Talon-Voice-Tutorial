How do I install a speech recognition engine to Talon for macOS?

A speech recognition engine allows Talon to recognize your voice commands and respond to them with action. This process should take 5 minutes.

Learn how to install Talon's free speech recognition engine:
Open Finder, go to Applications, and click on Talon to launch the application.
  1. In the menu bar at the top of your screen, click on the Talon icon.
  2. In the drop-down menu, click on Speech Recognition to open another drop-down menu.
  3. Under Wav2letter Engine, choose the latest speech recognition engine. Each engine's creation date is shown in parentheses by year, month, and day.
Congratulations, you have successfully added a speech recognition engine to Talon! When speaking, Talon will recognize and display your speech as text at the bottom of your screen.
Note: Talon may fail to recognize parts of your speech for several reasons, including the engine's lack of knowledge for non-native accents, "poor" pronunciation, a bad microphone, or a noisy environment. Check out actions you can take to improve Talon's speech recognition accuracy.
