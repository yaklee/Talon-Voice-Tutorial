How do I download voice commands to Talon?

Voice commands that are spoken to Talon allow you to control your computer. This process should take 5 minutes.

Before you can download voice commands, make sure to install Talon on your computer.

Talon has its own language system consisting of voice commands that, when spoken, will perform specific tasks.

Learn how to install the Talonhub/community voice command set on GitHub:

  1. In a web browser, go to the talonhub/community Github.
    1. Click the green Code button.
    2. In the drop-down menu, click Download ZIP.
    3. Once the download has completed, open Finder, go to Downloads, and double-click the community-main.zip to extract the voice command set's files.
      Once extraction has completed, a community-main folder will appear in your Downloads. Keep your Downloads open because you will need this folder later.
  2. Open Finder, go to Applications, and click on the Talon application.
    1. Once the application has launched, click on the Talon icon in the menu bar at the top of your screen.
    2. In the drop-down menu, go to Scripting and click Open ~/.talon.
  3. In the .talon folder, drag the community-main folder from your Downloads into the user sub-folder.
Congratulations, you have successfully downloaded voice commands for Talon!
Before you can use Talon with your voice, download a speech recognition engine for Talon.
