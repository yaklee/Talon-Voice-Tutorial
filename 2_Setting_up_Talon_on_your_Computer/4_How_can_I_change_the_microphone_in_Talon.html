How do I check my microphone in Talon?

The microphone allows your speech to be picked up by Talon's speech recognition engine.

If your microphone isn't working, then your voice commands won't either. Make sure that Talon is using the correct microphone for your setup:
  1. Open Finder, go to Applications, and click on the Talon application.
  2. Once the application has launched, click on the Talon icon in the menu bar at the top of your screen.
  3. In the drop-down menu, select Microphone, and choose a microphone option according to your setup.
Talon is now able to pick up on your voice! If you run into any trouble, check out the Talon community wiki's troubleshooting guide.
