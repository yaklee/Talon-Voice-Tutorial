    What is command, dictation, and sleep mode in Talon?

    Modes change how Talon interacts with your speech.

    You can switch between three different modes in Talon: command, dictation, or sleep.

    In command mode, Talon listens to your speech for voice commands and executes them in the order that they are spoken.

    In dictation mode, Talon listens to your speech and types it out as you speak. This mode is recommended when you want to transform large chunks of speech into text.

    In sleep mode, Talon is disabled. Therefore, the application will not listen to any speech or voice commands unless first prompted to wake up with the "Talon wake" voice command.
