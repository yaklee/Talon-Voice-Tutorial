How do I install Talon for macOS?

You must install Talon to your operating system to run the application. This process should take 5 minutes.

Before you install Talon, make sure your computer meets Talon's system requirements.
To install Talon:

  1. In a web browser, go to the Talon website.
    1. Click Download for macOS.
  2. Once the download has completed, open Finder, go to Downloads, and click the talon-mac.dmg disk file.
    1. In the pop-up window, drag the Talon icon into the Applications folder to add Talon to your computer.
  3. Open System Settings, go to Privacy & Security, and click Accessibility.
    1. Click the switch button to give Talon access to your computer. "Allow" is indicated by a blue switch button.
Congratulations, you have installed Talon onto your computer! The next time you launch the application, the Talon icon will appear in the menu bar at the top of your screen.
Note: Before you can use Talon with your voice, you need to download voice command files and install a speech recognition engine.
